Spider-Verse is a huge concept . In the comics , there are atleast a 100 spider-men like : Spider-Gwen , Miles Morales , Spider-Man Noir , Peny Parker , Spider-Ham , Spider-Man India ; to name a few . But we are going to focus on the MCM (Marvel Cinematic Multiverse)

During the final act of Spider-Man : No Way Home , Sony and Marvel kept their promise and based on our nostalgia , brought Tobey'Spider-Man and Andrew's Spider-Man to the big screen .

The Spider-Bros exchanged their experiences with each other which bonded them even closer with them .

Meeting Andrew's Spidey

As Doctor Strange's spell didn't work quite as planned so all the people who knew that Peter Parker was Spider-Man starting to coming to their universe from other universe . So wouldn't you know it ,our other two Spider-Men came from the Raimi Universe and from the Webb universe to our main MCU timeline.

Both of the Spider-Men came because of Ned using the "Sling Ring" while keeping his universe's Peter Parker in mine (Tom's Spidey) . At first both Ned and MJ were confused that Andrew's Spidey was not their Spider-Man , so tradtionally , they doubted him , asked him to that proove that he was a real Spidey . Then he prooved that he was infact similar to Tom's Spider-Man

Meeting Tobey's Spidey

After having showed up Andrew's Spidey , MJ encourages Ned to find their Peter . But we got to see Tobey's Spidey after , after almost 2 decades of him not playing the web-head . When Tobey's Spidey showed up , Ned even exclaimed ,"Great, this is some random guy." Then the both Spider-Man were actually a bit confused about each other , so to confirm , they shot webs at each other ; confirming that they were friendly.

At this point in Ned's grandma house , both the other Spider-Men said that they had felt that Tom's Spider-Man needed their help. Then following this , they both asked Ned and MJ to tell them where Peter would've gone to attain peace , and Ned and MJ told them where he would have .

Having Similarities

After MJ told both of them , where Peter would have gone , all of them rushed to that place , where Peter was mourning his aunt's death by the murderous hands of the Green Goblin. Ned and MJ were trying to console him but Peter sensed the presence of someone that would understand him ; they were Tobey's Spidey and Andrew's Spidey , jumping off of a huge building .

They told Peter that they would help him , understand him and that they were gone through from which Peter was going right now . Andrew's Spidey even told all of them that he couldn't save Gwen of his universe by exclaiming that she was,'..his MJ' This line was absolutely heart-breaking for us fans . Peter decided to team up with his older Spider-Bros.

Final battle

But Tobey's Spidey encouraged him by saying he was amazing. Also Peter told the other Spideys that he worked with the Avengers and had the experience of working in a team , to this both the other were confused by Avengers and Andrew's even asked him that if he worked in a band. Then they started the final battle.

So as Peter worked in a team multiple tims , he was the most experienced in team-working , he told the other Spider-Men that he was "Peter 1" Tobey's Spidey was "Peter 2" and Andrew's Spidey was "Peter 3"

Then after this , they all were able to coordinate their attacks perfectly , also cured many villains , bit as that box was destroyed by Green Goblin , all of the people that knew Spider-Man's identity were starting to come through and Doctor Strange couldn'd stop them.
